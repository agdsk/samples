## Code Samples

These folders contain sample selections from projects I've worked on.

### Demo-Booker

**Laravel 5.2**

An application that allowed consumers to book appointment times for a hands-on demonstration of a virtual reality
headset at retail stores in the US and EU. Brand ambassadors met with the customer to provide a guided tour of the
product. 

The application was a key component of the product launch as most consumers were experiencing virtual reality for the
first time. Hundreds of worldwide locations were included in the fully localized app, and thousands of brand ambassadors
conducted hundreds of thousands of demonstrations by the campaign's conclusion.

The app was also used with an alternative interface at two conferences with of thousands of attendees, allowing
conference attendees to schedule hands-on time with the headset at stations on the product demonstration floor.

### Training-Center

**Symfony 3.0**

The online class scheduling system for an American Heart Association training center operated by a large hospital
network. Hospital employees registered for required certification training courses and teachers conducted classes and
certification exams.

Thousands of employees of the hospital network are required to conduct continuous training and recertification from the
American Heart Association in courses like CPR and Basic Life Support. These courses are available at any training
center nationwide, but this training center was operated by the hospital itself.

Among the conveniences of operating their own training center, employees were able to authenticate with this Symfony app
using their network credentials from the hospital, and courses could be billed directly to the department manager of the
hospital.

All AHA training centers are required to be available to the public, so customers can also sign up with an email address
and password and pay for courses by credit card.
